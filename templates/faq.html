How do the insoles work?

Our insoles are clinically proven to significantly increase the circulation to the feet and lower legs. Because they contain a fluid, rather than foam or gel, they don't flatten or wear out. Instead the fluid is pushed up under the arch of the foot, providing gentle but firm support for flat feet and dropped arches and significantly reduce pain.
