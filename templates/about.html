An open science project based in Birmingham, United Kingdom. We're dedicated to promoting open access, open standards and open source.

Installables promotes elegant open-source software to solve current scientific challenges. We feel strongly that scientific progress is dependent on the availability of adequate tools unencumbered with licensing expensive or complicated licensing. You can browse the database to find free open tools to solve your scientific challenges.

Doables offers open access to scientific protocols enhanced with modern multimedia to improve understanding and share expertise. We collate protocols from across the web, with the author's permission and provide our modified versions under Creative Commons licensing CC-BY-SA. If you wish to submit your own protocols to the world contact us.
