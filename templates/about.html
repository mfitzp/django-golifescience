Installables promotes clear, elegant software to solve current scientific challenges. We feel strongly that scientific progress is dependent on the availability of adequate computing tools. In keeping with this philosphy we search out, and develop, research software that is available for unlimited use, without expensive or complicated licensing.

Doables offers open access to scientific protocols enhanced with modern multimedia to improve understanding and share expertise. We collate protocols from across the web, with the author's permission and provide our modified versions under Creative Commons licensing CC-BY-SA. If you wish to submit your own protocols to the world contact us.
