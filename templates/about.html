An open science project based in Birmingham, United Kingdom. We're dedicated to promoting open access, open standards and open source, with a particular focus on bioinformatics and big data.

We're building a directory of open-source software, tracking releases and publications to build a map of the key software currently at use in the field. We focus particularly on open source software, as we feel strongly that scientific progress is dependent on the availability of adequate tools unencumbered with expensive or complicated licensing. Alongside this we'll be providing protocols and 'how tos' for the most common scientific challenges, reviews of software in use and commentary on the latest developments. We've collated protocols from across the web, with the author's permission and provide our modified versions under Creative Commons licensing CC-BY-SA.

We're also very pro-user involvement, and will be adding features to allow submissions and user-feedback soon. If you wish to get in touch in the meantime, contact us.
